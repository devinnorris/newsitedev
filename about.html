Me...

I grew up in Minnesota and since birth have had an addiction to art. I started at a young age working along world class local artists like Bill Gossman, Berry Carlson, and many others. At 16, I started my own custom pottery business. At 17, I left the nest and started college at Northwest Nazarene University. I furthered my education and attended San Jose State and Eastern Washington University. Whether it be a new language I learn at home or computer coding at the University of Washington, I love to learn and to this day I continue to educate myself. While pursuing my education, I worked full time as a freelance artist, creating graphic work, paintings, logo design, marketing campaigns, murals, and ghost writing. I now call Seattle home continue to work as a freelance designer.
