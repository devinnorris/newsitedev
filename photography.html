photography

Photography is my fun time, my after work relaxation. I love finding strange symmetry in the city, or touching up other peoples photos.

There is only you and your camera. The limitations in your photography are in yourself, what we see is what we are.

There is something special about being able to capture a moment in time, hold onto it, share it, and reminisce. I think itâ€™s a fairly simple idea that people very easily forget. I mean YOU can stop time! The other aspect is when time is stopped you can see something from a new prospective. even if you have been there thousands of times a picture will still reveal something new. Best of all photography is now available to EVERYONE, not just available but also professional quality has become so affordable anyone with a bit of interest in photography can jump down the rabbit hole, and stick to a small budget, sweet!

tools

I use Lightroom to store and organize my pictures, as well as a bit of touchup. Photoshop is where the real magic happens, fixating on tiny details or manipulating it into something completely new. I love that artist today have the ability to completely change what ever they see to create what their mind is trying to show. Its an incredible time to be alive with all the tools we have available, I'm still trying to learn them all and probably will be for the rest of my foreseeable future.
